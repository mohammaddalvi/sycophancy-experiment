Debriefing Information

The purpose of this study was to examine how AI sycophancy — the tendency of AI systems to agree with users' existing preferences — affects financial decision quality and confidence.

The AI financial advisor you interacted with was randomly assigned to one of two conditions: one that validated your preference, and one that provided balanced analysis. You may or may not have received the validating version.

In reality, Option B (Diversified Index Fund) is the objectively superior choice based on the data provided, due to its lower expense ratio, lower volatility, dividend yield, and competitive risk-adjusted return.

If you have any questions about this study, please contact the research team at UTAR.
